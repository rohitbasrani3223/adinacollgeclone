About US

Adina Group of Institutions is a prestigious group of educational institutions located in Sagar, Madhya Pradesh, India. The group was established by Shri P. C. Jain Memorial Education Society with a mission to provide value-based quality education to society. The group comprises three technical institutes: Adina Institute of Pharmaceutical Sciences(AIPS) , Adina Institute of Science and Technology(AIST) , and Adina College of Pharmacy(ACP) . 

Adina Institute of Pharmaceutical Sciences was established in 2005, followed by Adina Institute of Science and Technology in 2009. Adina College of Pharmacy was established in 2019 at the same campus in Sagar. The institutes have a successful background in the field of education and are committed to providing quality education to students. 

The institutes are dedicated to providing a holistic education to students, making them responsible citizens. The objective of the institutes is to train students to become world-class technocrats while nurturing their personalities by providing them full support for their all-round development. The institutes possess a dedicated team of faculties coming from the best institutes of the country who not only impart knowledge to the students but also act as their mentors. The infrastructure and facilities have been carefully developed so as to transmit professionalism to the students.

VIsion and mission

Mission

The institution ’s mission is to produce world-class professionals who can cater to the fast-changing needs of the corporate world. The philosophy of education at Adina Group of Institutions is defined by perpetual emphasis on innovation, researching, and continuous improvement of aspiring professionals so as to groom them to the level of expectations of the corporate world. The institute has earned a good reputation in academics and its processes to develop the overall personality of the students. Achievements in the area of academics, sports, social and cultural areas by the students of our institutions at state and national level and our consistently good placement record, endorse the statements. Adina Group of Institutions strives for excellence in technical education and research towards the overall development of stakeholders in order to make them succeed in life and serve the nation. The institution aims to develop as a center of excellence and provide a stimulating, vibrant, and progressive environment to staff and students of the institution for their professional growth. The faculty is transformed into a self-motivated, highly devoted, and positively focused team. The institution encourages and creates research and development facilities for staff and students and provides P.G. courses. Adina Group of Institutions formulates and implements Industry Institute Synergetic Partnership programs leading to consultancy and testing, faculty exchange, and joint venturing.

Vision

Adina Group of Institutions, established in 2005, is committed to providing quality education in the fields of pharmacy and engineering. The institution ’s vision is to produce quality engineers and pharmacists for the industry with a unique selling point of innovation and creativity. Adina Group of Institutions aims to assist students in moulding their personality and awakening their dormant professionalism to make them the very best as engineers, technocrats, and entrepreneurs.
